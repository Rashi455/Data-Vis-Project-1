Explore the relationship between a lifestyle choice of physical activity and indicators of cardiovascular disease.

  • Select a health attribute from dropdown
  • Choose a type of county to filter
  • Drag accross the barcharts and scatterplot to select an area and then press the filter button that appears
  • Press reset all filters to reset any selections
  • Hover over counties on the maps, bars on the bar charts, and dots on the scatterplot to see specific info
